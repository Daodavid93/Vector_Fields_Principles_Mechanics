Vector field in two dimenensions is a function that assigns to each point (x,y) of the xy-plane a two-dimensional vector F(x,y). The standard notation is $$\vec{F}(x,y) = V(x,y).\vec{i} + Q(x,y).\vec{j} \frac{5}{4} $$

The most famous example is force on surface $$\vec{G}(x,y) = 0.\vec{i} -g.\vec{j}$$ where g = 9.8
